# 📖 Japanese Hiragana - Day 70

### Character: ね  
**Romaji:** ne  
**Pronunciation:** like 'ne' in 'net'  

✍️ **Stroke Order:**  
- 1: Horizontal line
- 2: Vertical line down
- 3: Loop to right

📝 **Mnemonic:** Looks like a nest 🪺 → sound 'ne'.  

📌 **Vocabulary Examples:**  
- ねこ (neko) = cat 🐱
- ねずみ (nezumi) = mouse 🐭
- ねだん (nedan) = price 💵

⚙️ **Grammar Usage:** Used in animals and numbers/prices.  

🎉 **Fun Fact:** ね is fourth in the 'na' row.  

🖊️ **Practice:**  
- Write ね 10 times ✍️
- Say ね aloud 5 times 🎤
