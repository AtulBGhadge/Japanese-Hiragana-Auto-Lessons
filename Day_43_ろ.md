# 📖 Japanese Hiragana - Day 43

### Character: ろ  
**Romaji:** ro  
**Pronunciation:** like 'ro' in 'robot'  

✍️ **Stroke Order:**  
- 1: Curve down and loop

📝 **Mnemonic:** Looks like a rolling stone 🪨 → sound 'ro'.  

📌 **Vocabulary Examples:**  
- ろうか (rouka) = corridor 🏢
- ろく (roku) = six 6️⃣
- ろば (roba) = donkey 🐴

⚙️ **Grammar Usage:** Used in numbers and objects.  

🎉 **Fun Fact:** ろ is fifth in the 'ra' row.
