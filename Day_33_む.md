# 📖 Japanese Hiragana - Day 33

### Character: む  
**Romaji:** mu  
**Pronunciation:** like 'moo' in 'moon'  

✍️ **Stroke Order:**  
- 1: Horizontal line
- 2: Curved stroke down

📝 **Mnemonic:** Looks like a cow 🐄 → sound 'mu'.  

📌 **Vocabulary Examples:**  
- むし (mushi) = insect 🐞
- むら (mura) = village 🏘️
- むね (mune) = chest ❤️

⚙️ **Grammar Usage:** Used in nature and body words.  

🎉 **Fun Fact:** む is third in the 'ma' row.

- Write む 10 times ✍️
- Say む aloud 5 times 🎤
